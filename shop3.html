DETAILS

What it is: A nightly eyelash conditioning serum infused with technology to enhance the appearance of length, volume + definition of eyelashes and eyebrows.

Why you need it: Lash durability can be enhanced with proper moisture and protection against breakage. Condition your eyebrows and eyelashes to achieve youthful, fuller-looking definition.

*Source Euromonitor International Limited; Beauty and Personal Care 2023 Edition, retail value RSP terms; all channels; Lash Serum including Lash Serum products sold as part of Sets and Kits.

WARNINGS: For external use. Keep out of reach of children. Avoid getting in eyes; if so, rinse with water. Do not use if pregnant or nursing, undergoing treatment for glaucoma or cancer, or if you have ever experienced conjunctivitis, dry eyes, eye infections, styes, irritation from other cosmetics applied in the eye area, or any eye-related disorder or illness. Some users have reported eye redness and itchiness, dry eyes, watering of the eyes, sensitivity, styes, inflammation of eyelid and eye, temporary darkening of skin around the eye area, lash loss and/or visible enhancement of hair around or outside the eye area. Stop use immediately if you experience these or other symptoms. If symptoms persist, seek medical attention. During the time Lash Boost has been on the market, R+F has received reports from some customers of other reactions, including iris discoloration, the appearance of droopy eyelids, and the exacerbation of meibomian gland dysfunction, among others.

HOW TO USE

Use once daily in the PM, on upper lash line only.

 

  1. Remove makeup and cleanse face.
  2. Dry eyelids and lashes completely.
  3. Apply serum only along the upper lash lines. Dip the brush once per eye and wipe excess product off the brush before applying.
  4. Gently wipe off excess serum from eyelids or lashes. Do not wash your face or eye area after applying. Wait ~90 seconds for serum to dry before going to sleep or applying other products near the eye area. Do not wash your face or eye area after applying. Common questions about the Rodan + Fields R + F Lash Boost

Can I use Rodan + Fields Lash Boost if I have eyelash extensions?

Rodan + Fields Lash Boost was designed for use on natural lashes. We do not recommend using it with eyelash extensions.

Can my teenage children use Rodan + Fields Lash Boost?.

We recommend that you discuss the use of Rodan + Fields Lash Boost by persons under 18 years old with a doctor.

Can Rodan + Fields Lash Boost be used on the lower lash line?

Do not use on the lower lash line. Applying Rodan + Fields Lash Boost on both the upper and lower lashes could lead to irritation in the eye area.

Can I use Rodan + Fields Lash Boost if I wear contact lenses?

Yes. Rodan + Fields Lash Boost has been found to be safe and non-irritating for contact lens wearers.

How long will a container of Rodan + Fields Lash Boost last?

If applied according to directions — once a day on upper lash line — one container should last approximately 2 months. We recommend replacing after 3 months from opening.

Is Rodan + Fields Lash Boost safe for my eyes?

Rodan + Fields philosophy is to create safe and beautifying formulations. We would not place a product on the market that does not meet our standards. Rodan + Fields Lash Boost has been found to be well tolerated and non-irritating for the great majority of users. However, if you think you might have a skin or eye sensitivity, we recommend conducting a patch test: after making sure your eyelids are clean and free of make-up, apply a small amount of Lash Boost to a portion of the eyelid margin near the upper eyelash line of one eye. if the eyelid skin appears normal and the eye shows no redness or irritation after 24 hours, then apply Lash Boost as directed but begin with a reduced frequency (e.g., every other day) for several days and gradually increase to every day, while monitoring for any tolerance issues. In some cases, the product may be better tolerated with a reduced frequency of application. if redness or irritation returns despite a reduction in frequency of application of the product, discontinue use of the product and consult with a medical professional.
